## Traceability process flow for requirements

- FSHLR/SysRS/TSR are part of DOORS Hi Server in project area (Ex: Zeeker)
- Using xChange tool, copy of the SWRS/HLR (for SMs) from CART shall be placed in CN CONV PF
- Further linking from the copy here, shall be possible (Ex: to TSR, SWCDD etc)
- Project config shall be updated with new sub module in DOORS project area(Zeeker) and linked
- RQM/Test specification document link shall be added
