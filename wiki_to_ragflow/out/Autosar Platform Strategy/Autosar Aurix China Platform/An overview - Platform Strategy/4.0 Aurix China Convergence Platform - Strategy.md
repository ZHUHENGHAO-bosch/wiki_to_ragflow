# 4.0 Aurix China Convergence Platform - Strategy

> Source: /spaces/CARSFW/pages/4120751015/4.0+Aurix+China+Convergence+Platform+-+Strategy
> Last modified: 2024-04-02T05:07:21.000+02:00

---

The Strategy is to create a China Aurix convergence platform, where platform components for SCC side will be implemented.

This will make project life easy, since we have always a PF Base to start with.

The plan is to reuse component from below two platforms

- Cerberus Platform - Where modules related to Aurix chip is available
- Hydra Platform - Reusable chip independent modules like LCM components are available

Both are above platform deliveries shall be present in common Convergence base platform owned by a team called CART

Our China Convergence Platform includes CART (Cerberus + Hydra) modules and few modules explicitly implemented for China convergence project requirements
