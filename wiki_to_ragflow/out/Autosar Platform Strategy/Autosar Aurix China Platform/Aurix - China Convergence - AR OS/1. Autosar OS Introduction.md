### Introduction

AUTOSAR OS is based upon the OSEK Operating System standard

OSEK - O ffene S ysteme und deren Schnittstellen für die E lektronik in K raftfahrzeugen →  In English "Open Systems and their Interfaces for the Electronics in Motor Vehicles"

### Architecture of the OSEK operating system

OSEK defines three processing levels:

- Interrupt level
- Logical level for scheduler
- Task level

![](../../../_images/1.%20Autosar%20OS%20Introduction/image-2023-5-6_13-43-5.png)

#### Priority Rules

1. Interrupts have precedence over tasks
2. The interrupt processing level consists of one or more interrupt priority levels
3. For task priorities and resource ceiling-priorities bigger numbers refer to higher priorities
