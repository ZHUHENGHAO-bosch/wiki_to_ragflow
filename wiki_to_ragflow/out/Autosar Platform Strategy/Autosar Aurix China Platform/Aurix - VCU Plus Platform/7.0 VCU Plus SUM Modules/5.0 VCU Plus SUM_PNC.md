## Overview

The Standard Utility Module, Partial Network Controller (SUM_PNC), is a software composition located in the Application Layer of the standardized architecture of AUTOSAR

SUM_PNC manages all the BSW services related to Partial Networks (PNs) for an ECU that is a member of any PN. SUM_PNC shall be implemented in all ECUs that are members of any PN

CLEA has no PNC requirement. Only GB has PNC requirement

CLEA electrical architectures do not support partial networks. Hence, the complete vehicle network wakes up and sleeps together

### SUM_PNC terms

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| PN Activation Criteria | a set of conditions that result in the activation of a PN. These conditions are typically a combination of local inputs and / or serial data signals |
| PN Deactivation Criteria | a set of conditions that result in the deactivation of a PN |
| Locally Active PN | a PN that is activated from within the ECU because its PN Activation Criteria was met |
| Remotely Active PN | a PN that is activated from outside the ECU |
| Activating ECU | an ECU assigned to monitor and process PN Activation Criteria. At least one ECU per PN is an Activating ECU, but there may be others (depends on the architecture deployment) |
| Activated ECU | an ECU is a member of an activated PN. All members of networks supporting PNs are capable of being Activated ECUs |

### Functionality of SUM_PNC

- For ECUs that are Activating ECUs, SUM_PNC contains the PN Activation Criteria and Deactivation Criteria per the "Global B PNC Activation Specification". SUM_PNC is the only SWC that interfaces with the BSW services to activate and deactivate Partial Networks
- For all ECUs that are Activated ECUs, SUM_PNC interfaces with the BSW services to track the activation status (active or inactive) of the PN.
- For all ECUs that are members of any PN, SUM_PNC provides a port that is used to indicate communication capability from SUM_PNC for each SW-C
- Collection of all input signals and input integrity signals (e.g. Rx_MonitoredSignalAvailability from SUM_SSM) that are relevant to Activation / Deactivation Criteria for the PN in which the ECU participates
- Collection of all conditional logic related to activation / deactivation criteria for the PN in which the ECU activates / deactivates
- Execute a sequence to fulfill the activation of a PN request
- Execute a sequence to fulfill the deactivation of a PN request
- Identify PN mapping to communication channel
