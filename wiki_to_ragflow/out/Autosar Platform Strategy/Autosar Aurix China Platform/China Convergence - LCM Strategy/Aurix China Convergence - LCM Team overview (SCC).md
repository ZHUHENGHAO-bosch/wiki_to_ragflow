### Roles and Responsibilities in Aurix Converence

LCM (Life Cycle Management) team is responsible for

- Implementation and Integration of all Lifecycle modules in SCC
- Support for all board bring up activities
- Analysis of any system level issue in projects
- Safety components in Life cycle management area
- Implementation of project specific requirements
