## Overview

This use case covers the safety integrity of the interrupt system. The associated hardware functions are divided into

- Interrupt request generation
- Interrupt handling
- Interrupt servicing

The configuration of the interrupt handling performed by the IR is done by the CPU through the SPB Bus.
