## Overview

This Use case considers the safety integrity of operations performed by a TriCore CPU on the EEPROM sub-function of the non-volatile data memory (DFLASH or DRRAM).

this use case, the following operations are being considered: 1. Load data from the EEPROM 2. Write data to the EEPROM
